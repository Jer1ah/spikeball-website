    The Basics

    • 2 VS 2

      Team A starts a point by hitting the ball down onto the net so it ricochets up towards Team B.

    • 3 TOUCHES

      Teams have up to three touches (think bump, set and spike) to return the ball into the net.

    • 360 PLAY

      After the serve, players can move or hit in any direction.

    • SCORING

      If a team can't return the ball onto the net with their 3 touches, their opponent scores 1 point. A shot that hits the ground, rim or bounces more than once on the net is no good. First team to 21 wins!
